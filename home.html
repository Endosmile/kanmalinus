This is an article about Heritage Polytechnic.

RECTOR'S WELCOME

Message

We at Heritage Polytechnic welcome you to our institution of excellence. We are commtted to raising students who will become excellent leaders in government and industries. Heritage Polytechnic is a private initiative of Dr. Emmanuel Ekott with support from NGOs, and companies operating within the Niger Delta Region of Nigeria. The polytechnic is located in a serene environment of over 30 hectres land mass in friendly community of Ikot Udota Eket, some three kilometers from ExxonMobil Airstrip Eket. The institution is about 10 minute drives from the heart of Eket town. The polytechnic started as a Continuing Education Centre in 1994 and grew to a college in 2003. Heritage Polytechnic has been approved, licensed by the Federal Government of Nigeria and accredited by the National Board for Technical Education (NBTE) since 2010 for award of National Diplomas and Higher National Diplomas. Heritage Polytechnic is currently the biggest private polytechnic in Nigeria with over 32 accredited programs.
